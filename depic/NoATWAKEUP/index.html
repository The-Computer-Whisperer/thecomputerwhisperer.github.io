NoATWAKEUP

  • NoATWAKEUP disables the ATWAKEUP (com.apple.atc.atwakeup) daemon from running. It automatically launches itself even after a reboot/restart. Disabling ATWAKEUP can help reduce battery drain in some circumstances, and no functionality is lost. There are no settings to configure.

  • REBOOT REQUIRED AFTER INSTALLATION. THIS INSTALLER WILL NOT AUTOMATICALLY REBOOT YOUR DEVICE!
